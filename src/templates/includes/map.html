Location, location, location! The map above indicates the Sauvignon Blanc vineyards ranked by Wine Enthusiast Magazine for the year 2016. Each marker represents a different appellation. By clicking on a marker the legend will update with relevant information for that locale. The container above the legend allows you to filter the selected vineyards further by rank - Best Rated: x>=90, Greatly Rated: 90>x>=85, Moderately Rated: x>85.
